Top 3 questions
Only 3 cities?
Distinct archetypes (coastal/radial/IT), ~15% of India's urban pop. LOCO measures the gap — we don't claim pan-India.
ResNet beats Swin — isn't Transformer better?
In-distribution yes; cross-city it reverses (Swin 79.1 > 77.1). Small data favours CNNs.
Just applying existing models?
Models are existing; the benchmark, LOCO, connected pipeline & reversal are the novelty.
